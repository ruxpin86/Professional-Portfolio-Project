About Me



Welcome to my first attempt at making a somewhat professional looking portfolio! For years I have been fascinated by the ability of programmers and developers to shape the world around us. I am originally from Connecticut, but made my way to Colorado via the Marshall Islands nearly ten years ago. I love snow sports and spending time hiking with my growing family!
